Our Story

Built on the Waters of Nigeria

Monica Marine Nigeria Limited was incorporated on 14th August 2002, with a clear vision to provide world-class maritime services. Our head office is located at 17 Liverpool Road, Apapa, Lagos — the heart of Nigeria's maritime industry.

From our early days as a ship chandling and supply company, we have grown into a full-service maritime organization serving some of the world's most respected shipping companies from Singapore, Greece, Dubai, UAE, Thailand and the United Kingdom.

Today, Monica Marine stands as a symbol of reliability, integrity, and professionalism — trusted by over 32 vessel operators and shipping lines operating across West Africa and beyond.
